As directed by UCEA, changes for clinical salaries are instructed on a nation-wide basis.  This change affects clinical consultants, clinical lecturers and clinical researchers and any staff that are on the NHS Agenda for Change scales.  The General Board and Council have agreed the clinical pay award and changes are effective from 1 April 2017.  The new clinical consultant salary scales confirmed by UCEA can be found below.
